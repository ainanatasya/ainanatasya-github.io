MY UNFORGETABLE EXPERIENCE

I visited my auntie in Langkawi, Kedah, last year. I went to Langkawi by myself, and the aim for my visit was to relieve my head after experiencing challenges in semester 3. My auntie, who runs a restaurant in Pulau Tuba, invited me to join her on a trip to Pulau Tuba after I arrived. Pulau Tuba is a lovely, isolated island located 5 kilometres southwest of Kuah Jetty. There is a traditional fishing town there, as well as uncommon wildlife and vegetation and a handful of homestays. Apart from the main Langkawi Island, it is also the only inhabited islet in the area, and can be reached through a 20-minute boat journey from Kuah Jetty. When they are not fishing or farming, villagers on the island have a very conventional lifestyle, venturing into the forest-covered hills to harvest medicinal plants, fruits, and honey.
